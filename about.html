Our Story

A legacy of authentic flavors and warm hospitality

Welcome to Sri Annapoorna Idly Kadai

Since 1995, we have been serving the heart and soul of South Indian cuisine to our beloved community. What started as a small family-run eatery has blossomed into a cherished destination for authentic flavors.

Our name, "Annapoorna," is inspired by the Hindu goddess of food and nourishment. True to this spirit, we believe in serving food that not only satisfies hunger but also nourishes the soul.

Every idli is steamed to perfection, every vada is fried to golden crispness, and every dish is prepared with recipes passed down through generations. We use only the finest ingredients, traditional cooking methods, and a whole lot of love.
